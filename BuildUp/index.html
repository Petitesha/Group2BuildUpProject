We're BuildUp

Buildup is a transformative program dedicated to lifting underprivileged individuals out of poverty through entrepreneurship education, mentorship and financial resources for startup. With a focus on vocational training and tailored resources, participants gain the skills and knowledge needed to start profitable businesses. The program's key strength lies in its comprehensive support system, offering ongoing guidance and encouragement to ensure the success of each entrepreneur. By empowering individuals to create their own opportunities.

Buildup rewrite the narrative of poverty, building a future of prosperity for all.
