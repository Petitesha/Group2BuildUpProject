Fostering entrepreneurial skills to eliminate poverty

Who we are:

(Tech Enthusiast), group 2 of Women Techsters cohort 3.3, created 2024, is an NGO that firmly believes that the entire nation, communities, businesses and groups can benefit from the implementation of programs and policies that adopt the notion of underpriviledge empowerment.

Our Vision

We are established to Eliminate Poverty by providing resources and assistance for underprivileged individuals to start profitable businesses through entrepreneurship education and mentorship.

Our Mission

Our central goal is to tackle the widespread problem of poverty by equipping people with the entrepreneurship skills they require, particularly those living in underprivileged areas. Acknowledging the shortcomings of conventional approaches to poverty alleviation, the strategy centers on promoting sustained economic independence via the establishment and maintenance of businesses. The initiative's dual goals of generating long-term economic benefit and eradicating poverty (SDG 1) are to be achieved by enabling people to become entrepreneurs.

Objectives

    Entrepreneurial Education

  • Provide easily available programs to equip people with the information and abilities needed to start and grow their own businesses.

    Support for Micro-Enterprises

  • Assist prospective entrepreneurs, especially those in low-income areas, in starting and expanding micro-enterprises by providing them with resources, financial support, and coaching.

    Job Creation

  • Encourage entrepreneurship to generate employment, support economic expansion, and give people and communities stable subsistence.

    Inclusivity and Diversity

  • Make inclusivity a top priority by emphasizing women and underrepresented groups, and make sure that a wide range of people may take advantage of entrepreneurial opportunities.

    Networking and Collaboration

  • Provide a forum for business owners to connect, work together, and exchange resources. This will help build a community that is helpful for sharing knowledge and expanding businesses.

Our Values

We have strong moral principles, being accountable for our missions and visions, and also being able to help, impact and impart others with various knowledge, skills and acquisitions, as well as experience so as to meet societal expectations and eradicate poverty.
